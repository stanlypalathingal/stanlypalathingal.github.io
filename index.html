Biography

  • Dr. (Fr) Stanly Wilson is an Associate Professor in the Department of Information Technology at St Vincent Pallotti College of Engineering & Technology Nagpur, Maharashtra, India.

  • His PhD (2021-2025) titled Blockchain Frameworks for Data Management and Provenance Tracking: Building Trust and Interoperability was under the supervision of Prof. Rajiv Ranjan and Dr. Tejal Shah (both from Newcastle University, UK).

  • His PhD contributed towards UK EPSRC funded projects EP/V042017/1 and EP/V042521/1.

  • His research interests include Distributed Ledger Technologies (DLT), Internet of Things, Cloud computing, Trust & Security, Provenance systems, and Machine Learning.

  • He is the recipient of the Newcastle University Overseas Research Scholarship (2021-2025).

  • Prior to his PhD, he has completed his MS in Data Science from Newcastle University (2020),

  • He completed his Masters in Computer Applications (MCA) from Christ University, Bangalore, India (2016-2019)

  • He completed his MA Philosophy from Karnatak University, Dharwad, India (2009-2012).
